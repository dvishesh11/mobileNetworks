B.E.(Electronics & Telecommunication)

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is a branch of engineering that integrates several fields of electrical engineering and computer science required to develop and design telecommunication systems. It also involves the transmission of information across the channel. Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is a field that involves complex electronic apparatus, circuits, and equipments that help in transmitting information across long distances. The work of an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer can be found in computer industries, communication sector, aerospace industry, and many other industries.
