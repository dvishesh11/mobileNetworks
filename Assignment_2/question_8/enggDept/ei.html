B.E.(Electronics & Instrumentation)

Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering is a specialized branch of Electrical Engineering. It deals with the study of electrical, electronics and instrumentation engineering. The course focuses on the principle and operation of measuring instruments, which are used in the design and configuration of automated systems. The course also covers the design, development, installation, manufacturing, and maintenance of electrical and electronic instruments and equipment. The course is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the areas of electrical and electronics engineering.
