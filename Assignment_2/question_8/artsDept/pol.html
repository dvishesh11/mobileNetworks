B.A.- Political Science, Sociology, History

Political Science, Sociology, and History are three distinct disciplines that are often studied together due to their interconnectedness. Political Science is the study of politics, government, and political behavior. Sociology is the study of society, social institutions, and social relationships. History is the study of past events, people, and societies. Studying these three disciplines together provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the political, social, and historical forces that shape our world.
