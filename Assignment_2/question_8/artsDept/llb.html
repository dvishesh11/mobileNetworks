B.A. LLB. (Hons.) (5 Years Full-time Programme)

BA LLB is a five-year integrated course that offers a comprehensive understanding of the legal system in India. The course is designed to provide students with a wide range of legal subjects, such as constitutional law, criminal law, family law, and more. The course also includes subjects from the arts stream, such as history, economics, and political science, to provide students with a well-rounded education.
